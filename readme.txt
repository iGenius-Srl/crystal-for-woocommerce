== Description ==

crystal boosts your e-business results by helping you run campaigns that work and keeping you updated on e-shop results.

crystal works as your virtual advisor for digital marketing. By installing the crystal for WooCommerce plugin, crystal’s Artificial Intelligence learns from your e-shop data and tells you (literally!) when it’s the best moment to run a campaign.

It’s easy as pie: crystal autoselects products for you, gives you a range of budgets and placements to choose from and automatically promotes your products to the right targets for you.

You can talk or write to crystal at any time – crystal may suggest you a Facebook and Instagram campaign or a flash sale to boost your products, based on their previous performance.

With crystal also be able to monitor campaign results, product sales, customer demographics and more, while and after ads run.

crystal will take care of everything while you enjoy your e-shop growing the way you need, so try it today.


== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP version 5.2.4 or greater (PHP 5.6 or greater is recommended)
* MySQL version 5.0 or greater (MySQL 5.6 or greater is recommended)
* WordPress 4.4+
* SSL certified  WooCommerce url (https format is mandatory)
* WooCommerce 2.9+

= Installation guide =

1. Download the crystal for WooCommerce plugin
2. Upload the crystal-for-woocommerce.zip file to the /wp-content/plugins/directory
3. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress

Please note: only crystal premium users can fully enjoy all the plugin’s features. If you’re not premium yet, upgrade now!
